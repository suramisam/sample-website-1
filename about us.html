History

With a vision to empower the youth in the Information Communication Technology (ICT) age, Informatics Institute of Computer Studies (IICS), now known as Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT), was established in Colombo, Sri Lanka in 1990.

IIT Campus

IIT Celebrates 30 years of Educational Excellence with resounding success in Information Technology and Business Education. We have groomed more than 5000 successful graduates during the past three decades with a growing network of alumni in Sri Lanka and around the globe.

Student Life

IIT offers a stellar platform for all capabilities and inaterests within its 45 clubs and societies, thus, musicians,sportsman, fitness maniacs,posets, artistsand even techies are afforded a niche community to thrive.

Transfer Programmes

Some of our programmes offer the exciting opportunity of transferring to the international university that you are enrolled in. Depending on your degree specialization and chosen university of affiliation, transfers can occur at the successful completion of first or second year.

Convocation

The most significant moment of our students' academic journey with us, is the IIT convocation week, every December. Every year,IIT witnesses hundreds of its students soar out into the real world- adorned with the best in enducation and practice.
