History

With a vision to empower the youth in the Information Communication Technology (ICT) age, Informatics Institute of Computer Studies (IICS), now known as Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT), was established in Colombo, Sri Lanka in 1990.

IIT Campus

IIT Celebrates 30 years of Educational Excellence with resounding success in Information Technology and Business Education. We have groomed more than 5000 successful graduates during the past three decades with a growing network of alumni in Sri Lanka and around the globe.
